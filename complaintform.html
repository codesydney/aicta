How to lodge a Complaint

Step 1: Check out the fact sheet on how to make a complaint.

Step 2: Choose how you would lodge a complaint
There are two ways to lodge a complaint, either via Online Form (Step 3A) or via Post (Step 3B) below.

Step 3A Lodge complaint via Online Form
Click the link below to go to Fair Trading Online Complaint Form

Click this link to lodge the complaint online via Fair Trading's Associations Complaint Form

Step 3B Lodge complaint via Post
Download and fill out the form below and post to Registry Services, PO Box 22, Bathurst NSW 2795.
