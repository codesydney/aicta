About Us

Welcome to AICTA

I am honoured to welcome you to the association of REGISTERED TAX AGENTS

I personally believe that it was a satisfying journey for you and me to attain our professional careers. As a tax lawyer or a professional accountant … the journey to the ultimate qualifications is your registration a TAX AGENT.

The Tax Act is clear about registration as a tax agent. Considering that even though you are a CPA or a topnotcher accountant, it would be illegal or unlawful for you to provide taxation services if you are not under the supervision of a tax agent.

As a tax agent, we must be honest, integrity, confidential, and competent in the work of a tax professional. We must be a trusted tax adviser. We have an obligation is to ensure that the Tax Laws are applied accordingly.

First and foremost, we will continue to assist the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) in the implementation of the tax laws of the land.

I thank you for your interest in joining AICTA.

Mario Pleno CPA Life, AICTA
